Table of contents

Introduction to oral and written comprehension and production through the integration of skills linguistic-communicative. Emphasis on orality, taking into account the academic-professional specificities of the area and addressing socio-cultural aspects of the English language.

Goals

To present the resources of the English language aiming to develop the four skills, reading, listening, writing and speaking. Support the activities of building blogs and websites Internet foreseen for the semester. Students should be able to: introduce themselves, give personal and professional information; understand numbers in different contexts; ask questions simple and respond on everyday life and business communication such as writing emails and answer a phone call; extracting information from technical texts specific to the area; understand basic pronunciation differences.
